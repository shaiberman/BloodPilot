In this study, you will be asked to repeat tasks on 2 separate days!
The second day will be slightly shorter - excluding 3 questionnaires.

Importantly, the two days should be done in different hunger states.
Therefore, if today you accept and perform the HIT having eaten in the last 3 hours, we ask that when you do part 2 of this task you will do it before eating breakfast (or lunch in case you do not eat breakfast).
Alternatively, if you accept the HIT now and you haven't eaten in several hours, we ask that you do part 2 of this task not long after a meal.

Part 2 of this task will start tomorrow, and will be available in the next few days.

Please do NOT accept this HIT if you are not willing or able to repeat the study in the following days. Thank you for participating!
